FAQs

Questions, answered simply.

How do I know which extension method is right for me?

Your consultation covers hair type, lifestyle, colour, and desired result so the most suitable method can be recommended.

Will extensions damage my natural hair?

When applied, maintained, and removed correctly, extensions can be worn comfortably with your natural hair health in mind.

How often will I need maintenance?

Most clients return every 6 to 10 weeks, depending on the method, natural growth, and home care routine.
